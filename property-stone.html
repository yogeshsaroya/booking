One-of-a-kind boutique apartment located just 3 blocks from SoHo & Hyde Park Village, and just a few minutes to downtown Tampa. Super comfortable and packed with amenities.

The Location

Just minutes to downtown Tampa, Amalie Arena, Raymond James Stadium, and the Straz Center. The property is 3 blocks from South Howard Street (SoHo) - Tampa's premier dining and nightlife district - and Hyde Park Village's boutique shopping.

Walk to the famous Bayshore Boulevard, the longest continuous sidewalk in the country, for gorgeous bay views and waterfront strolls.
