About This Space

Handcrafted with love and attention to detail, The Cedar showcases the beauty of natural materials. Real cedar, quartzite stone, and thoughtful design elements create a serene rustic-chic retreat in the heart of Hyde Park.

Every element in this space was personally designed and built by your host. From the floating wood accents to the 12-inch rainfall showerhead, you'll experience true craftsmanship at every turn. It's small but spacious-feeling, cozy yet sophisticated.

What You'll Love

  • Quartzite stone and real cedar finishes throughout - truly one-of-a-kind
  • 12-inch rainfall showerhead for a spa-like bathing experience
  • Lighted floating wood elements creating unique ambiance
  • 55" TV with soundbar and subwoofer for entertainment
  • Private fenced backyard perfect for morning coffee or pet playtime
  • Free private parking - no circling the block
  • Pet-friendly at no extra charge - we love your furry friends!

The Location

Just a short three-block walk to SoHo and Hyde Park Village - Tampa's epicenter for dining, shopping, and nightlife. The location offers the perfect balance of walkable urban convenience and peaceful residential charm.

Minutes to downtown Tampa, Raymond James Stadium, Amalie Arena, and the beautiful Bayshore Boulevard waterfront. Florida's beaches are just 40 minutes away, and Orlando's theme parks are an easy 90-minute drive.

Perfect For

  • Nature lovers who appreciate organic materials and natural design
  • Couples seeking a romantic, intimate hideaway
  • Pet parents who refuse to compromise on style for their pets
  • Design enthusiasts who appreciate handcrafted details
  • Anyone who values authenticity and artisanal craftsmanship
