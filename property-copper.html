About This Space

Bold and beautiful, The Copper is our maximalist masterpiece. Rich copper accents, vibrant colors, and eclectic design create an unforgettable atmosphere just steps from Tampa's best dining and nightlife.

This isn't your typical rental - it's a carefully curated experience. Every detail has been considered, from the dramatic color palette to the premium entertainment system. If you appreciate design that makes a statement, you'll love The Copper.

The Location

Located in the heart of Hyde Park, you're a short stroll from South Howard Street's acclaimed restaurants, craft cocktail bars, and boutique shopping. The property offers the perfect blend of vibrant neighborhood energy and peaceful retreat.

Quick access to downtown Tampa, sports venues, and Tampa's beautiful beaches. Everything you want to experience in Tampa is within easy reach.
